The AI Evolution

Five levels of capability — each one building on the last.

1
Large Language Models
AI that answers in natural language
2
Retrieval (RAG)
AI that finds and cites sources
3
Multimodal AI
AI that sees, hears, and connects
4
AI Agents
AI that plans, acts, and collaborates
5
Agentic Architecture
AI that scales with memory and governance
The through-line: One question — "What are the main challenges facing Caribbean postal services regarding terminal dues and cost recovery?" — travels through all five levels, gaining depth, sources, and actionability at each step.

The CREATIVO Framework

Eight dimensions to structure your communication with AI. Better prompts → better results.

Dimension What It Does Postal Example
C Context The background — the why behind your question "I work at a Caribbean postal service classified as Group 3 under the UPU terminal dues system."
R Role What expertise the AI should adopt "Act as a senior policy research analyst specializing in international postal economics."
E Example Show it what good output looks like "Structure findings like a delegation briefing: executive summary, evidence, comparative analysis, recommendations."
A Audience Who the output is for — adjusts complexity and tone "Senior postal officials and government delegates who understand the sector but need data-backed arguments."
T Task The specific action — a clear verb "Analyze whether current UPU terminal dues rates cover the actual cost of processing inbound international parcels."
I Instructions Rules, constraints, format, what NOT to do "Use only verifiable sources. Compare at least 3 peer countries. Maximum 3 pages. Formal tone."
V Voice How should it sound? "Diplomatic, evidence-based, authoritative — suitable for an international delegation document."
O Objective The ultimate goal — what success looks like "Produce a briefing paper strong enough to influence the terminal dues reclassification discussion."

Full CREATIVO Prompt Example

CREATIVO Prompt — Terminal Dues Briefing C — CONTEXT: I work at a Caribbean postal service classified as Group 3 under the UPU terminal dues system. Our inbound parcel volumes have grown 40% in 3 years, but terminal dues revenue has not kept pace with processing costs. We are preparing for a UPU regional meeting on reclassification. R — ROLE: Act as a senior policy research analyst specializing in international postal economics and UPU regulatory frameworks. E — EXAMPLE: Structure findings like a delegation briefing: executive summary, evidence with data points, comparative analysis with peer countries, and actionable recommendations. A — AUDIENCE: Senior postal officials and government delegates who understand the sector but need data-backed arguments for negotiation. T — TASK: Research and analyze whether current UPU terminal dues rates cover the actual cost of processing inbound international parcels for Caribbean SIDS countries, and recommend a negotiation strategy. I — INSTRUCTIONS: - Use only verifiable sources with citations - Include specific cost/revenue data where available - Compare at least 3 peer countries in similar positions - Maximum 3 pages for the main briefing - Formal, institutional tone throughout V — VOICE: Diplomatic, evidence-based, authoritative — suitable for an international delegation document. O — OBJECTIVE: Produce a briefing paper strong enough to influence the terminal dues reclassification discussion at the upcoming UPU regional meeting.
Key insight: The same question with CREATIVO produces dramatically better results. The framework works with any AI tool — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity. It’s not about the tool. It’s about how you communicate with it.

Getting Started

A step-by-step workflow you can follow tomorrow morning.

1

Understand the Landscape (5 min)

Open Perplexity AI (Academic Mode). Ask your research question in plain language. You'll get a structured answer with cited sources. Copy the key findings.

2

Analyze Your Own Documents (15 min)

Open Google NotebookLM. Upload your internal reports, UPU documents, and financial data. Ask cross-referencing questions. Generate an Audio Overview for your team.

3

Generate a Research Report (10 min)

Open ChatGPT Deep Research or Gemini Deep Research. Paste a detailed prompt (use CREATIVO). The AI autonomously searches 100+ sources and produces a full report with citations.

4

Build Your Argument (20 min)

Combine the outputs from Steps 1–3. Use Claude or ChatGPT to draft your briefing paper, position document, or presentation. Every claim is sourced. Every comparison is documented.

Step Tool Time Output
1. Landscape Perplexity 5 min Sourced answers to key questions
2. Your data NotebookLM 15 min Document synthesis + audio briefing
3. Research report Deep Research 10 min 15–20 page cited report
4. Briefing paper Claude / ChatGPT 20 min 2-page delegation brief
Total ~50 min Complete briefing package
